## Root cause — the diagnostics converged

- **hallucination_score** (#112, #114): the panel genuinely fabricates
  numbers — systemic across all 7 voices, a seed-then-cascade pattern. The
  grounding gate *detects* every ungrounded figure but only floors confidence
  + appends a blocker — it **never scrubs the figure from `turns[]`/
  `key_drivers`**, so the judge still reads it and still penalizes. S36-WS1's
  truncation diagnosis was *inverted*; S36 fixed the wrong thing.
- **Ruled out:** retrieval is sound (#113 — healthy 15-chunk slates);
  context-overflow / null-turn cascade is absent (#114 — 0 degraded turns,
  ~3.5K tokens vs 128K window); the judge is trustworthy (#112).
- **verdict_accuracy** (#112): a coordinator bug — it cannot map
  ACT/PASS/DEFER on rotation-framed questions. NOT #92.
- **confidence_calibration**: a marginal miss; likely distorted by the gate
  slamming confidence to 0.00 on every fire. May fall out of WS1.
- **D1 (structural):** `must_not_hallucinate` demands grounding in
  `market_data`/`paysh_live` chunks that never reach the panel — part of the
  penalty is currently unwinnable as written.
