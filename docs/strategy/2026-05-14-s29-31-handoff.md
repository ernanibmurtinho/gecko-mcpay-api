## §5 — Notes on the canary failure

The Tier-1 canary failed on D3 (citations_grounded) with "phantom citations: 5 of 10 — markers [3][6][8][9][10] never appear in any persona content." This is a calibration artifact of the canary's D3 check, not a real regression:

- `build_citations_from_chunks(chunks)` emits one citation entry per retrieved chunk (the contract is "what we looked at," not "what was inline-cited").
- D3's phantom-marker check assumed personas would cite roughly every chunk they were shown. At top_k=5 the basic-tier panel inlines ~4-5 markers (~all). At top_k=10 it still inlines ~5-7 markers, leaving 3-5 "phantom" cites.
- The rubric N=10 (the binding V1 gate) does not use this check — judge_notes treat the citations array as "consulted sources" and grade relevance, not inline-coverage.

**Follow-up:** the canary's D3 phantom-marker check needs recalibration for top_k=10 (e.g. accept `phantom_marker_count ≤ top_k // 2`). Not dispatched this sprint. Filed as candidate S29 #34-eval.
