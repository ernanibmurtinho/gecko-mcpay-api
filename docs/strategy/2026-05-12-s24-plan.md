## 1. The headline finding (do not skip)

`ai-ml-engineer` adversarial review of the verdict surfaced a sprint-altering gap. Verbatim:

> "Zero closed-loop eval on actual trade verdicts. `tests/eval/suites/` holds general / saas / crypto-startup suites scoring ship/kill/pivot on company-building ideas. There is no `defi_trade_suite.json`, no historical-outcome rubric, no fixture where 'act on Kamino USDC vault at date X' has a known 30d-forward P&L. We are charging $0.75 for a verdict whose verdict-correctness has never been measured against its native task."

> "3 of 5 analysts (technical, sentiment-via-news-recency, fundamental-via-TVL) require real-time market data the corpus does not contain. Canon (Marks/Damodaran/Berkshire) is timeless wisdom literature — useful for the risk_manager and strategist, useless to the technical_analyst. The closing-line regex forces a verdict token regardless. A DeFi user will spot the mismatch in one question."

**Implication for the sprint:** we cannot flip `X402_MODE=live` and invite 10 real users to pay $0.75/verdict on a panel whose technical_analyst hallucinates price levels from Howard Marks memos. The eval suite + Pyth market-data wiring becomes the new critical path. Live flip is gated on eval pass.

This reorders the sprint. Workstreams below reflect the reshuffle.

## 2. Sprint goal (revised)

**Ship a falsifiable trade-verdict eval suite + market-data grounding fix, then invite ten outside-network users to install, pay live x402, and complete a verdict cycle — with no support DMs.**

Two halves of the same outcome:
- **Quality half:** the verdict must be defensible to a skeptical DeFi user, not just a hackathon judge.
- **Reach half:** ten installs from outside our network, ≥1 returning within 7 days.

If we hit the reach metric but fail the quality bar, **we have a vanity success** — first users churn, NPS goes negative, future GTM is poisoned.

## 4. Workstreams (7 lanes)

Reordered from the staff-engineer draft to put the eval+grounding gate first.

### WS-A — Trade-verdict eval suite + market-data grounding (NEW, blocks live flip)

**Owner:** ai-ml-engineer + data-engineer

**Problem:** No closed-loop eval on trade verdicts. 3/7 voices structurally bluff without market data.

**Ships:**
- `tests/eval/suites/defi_trade_suite.json` — 40 historical Solana DeFi decisions (12 Kamino vault entries, 8 Drift perp directional calls, 8 Jupiter LST rotations, 6 Jito MEV-tip bands, 6 Sanctum/Marinade unstake-vs-hold). Date range 2024-Q3 → 2025-Q4. Each fixture freezes corpus to chunks dated ≤ T.
- Independent outcome script — reads Pyth historical / on-chain price data, never shown to panel. Two reviewers re-label 10% for ground-truth audit.
- Pass bar: `act` verdicts profitable at 30d ≥ 58%; `pass` verdicts avoid drawdowns ≥ -15% at ≥ 70%; `defer` rate < 30%; Brier score ≤ 0.30. Budget: $30 to run full pro-tier sweep.
- New `provider_kind=market_data` source: Pyth historical + DefiLlama TVL snapshots. `freshness_tier=hot`. Wired into `retrieve_trade_corpus_chunks` via existing canon-style $match (Pattern F).
- Prompt patch: `technical_analyst` collapsed to `macro_regime_analyst` OR explicit "abstain when no market-data citation available" instruction.

**Why this sprint:** without this, every other workstream is shipping a verdict of unknown quality at scale.

## 6. Definition of done — Sprint S24

Sprint is **complete** when ALL of these are true:

- [ ] **Quality:** WS-A 40-fixture suite has run; report committed to `docs/eval/2026-05-2X-s24-trade-suite.md`. Brier ≤ 0.30 documented OR prompt patch applied + suite re-run shows improvement.
- [ ] **Settlement:** `X402_MODE=live` for `/trade_research` in prod; ≥ 20 real mainnet tx settled by founder/cofounder; ≥ 5 from outside-network users.
- [ ] **Reach:** ≥ 10 unique installs from outside founder network; ≥ 5 of those complete at least one paid `gecko_trade_research`; ≥ 1 returns within 7 days.
- [ ] **Trust:** zero support DMs from outside-network users completing first verdict. (If they DM us asking how to install, that's a fail — fix the friction, don't reply.)
- [ ] **Runtime:** `bb trade-agent` CLI ships on PyPI v0.2.26+; gecko-claude `gecko-trade-agent` skill published; ≥ 3 strategy specs in `examples/` (Kamino DCA + Drift momentum-fade + Jito LST rotation).

Sprint is **failed-with-grace** if Quality + Settlement land but Reach misses. → S25 reframes around distribution.

Sprint is **structurally-broken** if Quality misses. → Pull back to stub, no live flip, retro post-mortem.
