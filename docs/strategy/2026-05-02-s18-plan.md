## 1. Wedge — what we lead with after S17

**Pick: (a) + (b) fused — "adversarial-debate verdict + grounded dissent, packaged as a tradeable judgment."**

Why not pure (b) "tradeable judgments as commodity": the commodity framing is the *wrapper*, not the moat. Anyone can put a price on a verdict; what they can't easily replicate is a 5-agent debate over a session corpus where dissent is grounded in cited chunks across heterogeneous providers (web, Bazaar, Arxiv, twit.sh). Per Pattern D, orchestration is table stakes — the verdict *shape* (gap-classified, evidence-floor-clamped, dissent-preserving) is the defensible artifact. The commodity layer makes that artifact *legible* to a market.

Why not (c) reputation: not yet load-bearing. Bucketed reputation matters when there are >10 contributors and a leaderboard surface — neither exists in S18.

**One-sentence wedge for PRD/skill.md/splash:**
> Gecko produces grounded, adversarial verdicts on pre-ideas — a judgment you can buy, sell, or stake on.

## 3. Risks & gotchas

1. **v1_block tear-out is S19 territory but the pro-debate prompt depends on its "always-render-heading" contract.** S18-V1BLOCK-AUDIT-01 (#10) is the recon ticket so we don't sleepwalk into a regression in S19. Do NOT remove v1_block in S18.

2. **Tradeable-judgment requires settlement, and x402 facilitator is stub-only for now.** S18-TRADEABLE-SURFACE-01 is spec-only on purpose. Live settlement for verdict purchase is web3-eng territory and a 2-week slice minimum. Don't let demo enthusiasm pull live settlement into S18.

3. **`gecko-claude` repo doesn't exist on disk.** Per S17 plan notes. `app.geckovision.tech/skill.md` is live (verified) but `install.sh` execution is gated on whoever owns the static hosting. S18-INSTALL-DISTRIB-01 must verify the upload path, not just the script.

4. **Verdict-hash determinism is harder than it looks.** Corpus fingerprinting must be order-stable; embed cache hits change retrieval order subtly. S18-VERDICT-HASH-01 (#9) needs to hash the *retrieved* citation set after rerank, not the corpus pre-rank, otherwise the hash is non-deterministic across reruns.

5. **Eval threshold drift after S17 hybrid reranker is unknown.** S18-EVAL-GAP-01 (#4) might surface a regression. If verdict-accuracy drops below 0.7, rolling back the reranker is correct — the wedge claim is grounded dissent, not aggressive ranking.

## 5. Success metric

**One number:** Shipathon demo executes in **<90 seconds end-to-end from `Read app.geckovision.tech/skill.md`** to rendered verdict, with the verdict containing **≥3 distinct `provider_kind` citations** (e.g. one web, one bazaar, one arxiv-or-twitsh) and a **stable verdict hash** printed in the footer.

If both conditions hold on the user's own meta-idea ("judgment as a tradeable commodity"), S18 succeeded. If the verdict on that idea is GO or REFINE (not PIVOT/NO), the wedge framing is dogfood-validated. If it's PIVOT, ship the pivot — that's the product working.
