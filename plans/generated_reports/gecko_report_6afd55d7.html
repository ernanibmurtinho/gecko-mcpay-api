Business Plan

problemBuilders and innovators face fragmented and costly access to categorized, high-quality signals from diverse knowledge sources for pre-ideation and market research.
ICPStartup founders, product teams, market researchers, and AI agents seeking aggregated, categorized, and continuously enriched knowledge bases for early-stage ideation and decision-making.
solutionGecko aggregates signals from Tavily, twit.sh (X), Bazaar, and pay.sh, classifies them into seven knowledge categories, and offers paid retrieval endpoints via MCP/x402 on Solana, with internal multi-agent debate enriching the knowledge base to reduce cost-per-call over time.
marketThe market includes startup ecosystems, product development teams, AI-driven research platforms, and decentralized knowledge services leveraging blockchain infrastructure like Solana.
channelsDirect sales to startups and AI companies, partnerships with decentralized app builders on Solana, developer communities, and integrations with AI agent platforms.
business_modelPay-per-call via x402 micropayments on Solana for external agents accessing categorized knowledge retrieval endpoints; bulk credit pack ($10 → 1.5M tokens) as the only prepay shape — explicitly NOT subscription. Internal usage enriches data to lower costs and improve value.
risks
  • Dependence on quality and availability of external data sources (Tavily, twit.sh, Bazaar, pay.sh).
  • Complexity of maintaining accurate classification across seven knowledge categories.
  • Competition from existing market intelligence and data aggregation platforms.
  • Technical challenges in scaling multi-agent debate and continuous enrichment.
  • Regulatory or compliance risks related to data usage and blockchain operations.
